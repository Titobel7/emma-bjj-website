ABOUT EMMA

From shy little girl to national champion

Emma started Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in May 2022, when she was only five years old. At the beginning she was shy, introverted and insecure, but martial arts completely transformed her life.

Through discipline, consistency and passion for competition, Emma evolved into one of the strongest young promises in the AJP national circuit in Spain.

Today, at only 9 years old, she is currently ranked #1 in the national AJP Spain ranking in the Gray Belt -36kg category.

Emma trains every day at Fightzone Costa Blanca in La Nucía (Alicante), where she continues building her dream: becoming national champion.
